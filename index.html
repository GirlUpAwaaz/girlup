ONGOING PROJECTS

Project Awaaz

This World Mental Health Day, our club launched a new project called “Project Awaaz” with the slogan “Meri Kahaani, Meri Awaaz”. Our aim for this project is to provide a platform to all the fighters, who have faced hardships and come out stronger, to share the story of their battles through us, either named or unnamed. The story can be shared through the link provided in the bio of our Instagram page. The link will direct one to a google form where they can write their story. People can also share their story in a video format if they’re comfortable with it. For this, they can simply attach a drive link of the video in the form. 

Share your story! 

SUCCESSFUL PROJECTS

Project सहायता

In September, our team successfully conducted Project सहायता, an old clothes donation drive, in which our main aim was to donate as much as we can and do our best to help out little children. We asked people for online donations through which we were able to raise more than adequate funds for our drive.
We went to Nishulk Pathshala, a school in a slum area, where we donated clothes, shoes, bags, and stationery items like books, pencil boxes, crayons, drawing books,etc., to the children and also conducted general knowledge related games and quizzes for them and rewarded them with goodies.
The happiness on the faces of the children made our drive a successful one.

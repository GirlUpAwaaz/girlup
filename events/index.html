Project सहायता

Presenting Project सहायता, an initiative of Girl Up Awaaz, aimed to collect clothes and other kind of donations from the privileged class to distribute them to those in need.

We invite you to participate in this drive by donating from your part and be a part of a good cause. People who will be donating, will get featured in our posts and stories as a token of appreciation.
Also, If you are willing to make a monetary donation, contact the zonal representatives respectively. That fund will be of great help to us.

PCOS Live Event

Girl Up Awaaz hosted a PCOS Live Seminar featuring Gynaecologist Dr Sheetal Agarwal and Nutritionist Dr Gauri Anand. Topics such as Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome, Symptoms, Criteria for diagnosis, Diagnosis of polycystic ovaries in adolescence, Management of PCOS and Nutrition and diet to be taken for better treatment of PCOS were picked up.
The Event was held on 25th of August on Microsoft Teams.

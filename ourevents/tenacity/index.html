UPDATE REGARDING EVENT TENACITY!

Updated 7 PM, 28th October, 2021.
A very big thanks to you if you are a part of Tenacity. Your support really matters a lot for us!
There is a change in the schedule.

The Event will be held on 30th of October.
Schedule of events:
1. Art Competition: 4:00 - 4:20 PM
2. Singing Competition: 4:20 - 5:00 PM
3. Poetry Writing: 5:00 - 6:00 PM

All the other competitions have been cancelled due to some reasons.
The following events have been cancelled:
1. Dancing Competition
2. Kaun Banega Champion (KBC)
3. Fashion Show
4. Photography Competition
5. Rapping Competition

Those who participated in the above events can either get a participation certificate for participating, or could get a refund.
Though we would like to tell you that we are working for a very noble cause, and your funds are very important for us.
Also if you have not yet submitted your entries, please do mail us your entries (girlupawaaz@gmail.com)!

Girl Up Awaaz is holding a two-day fundraising event on 30th and 31st of October to raise funds for a menstrual kit donation drive for the underprivileged menstruators.

In this event we will be holding 7 different competitions:
1. Singing/Rapping
2. Dancing
3. Art
4. Photography
5. Fashion Show
6. KBC (Kaun Banega Champion)
7. Poetry Writing.

To participate, you’ll have to fill the form and give a minimal fee of ₹50.
To witness the event and become a member of the audience, you’ll have to fill the form and give a minimal fee of ₹20.

If you want, you can also donate more than the required fee to help us with our cause.

Competitions!

SINGING/RAPPING COMPETITION

In this competition, the participants will have to mail us a pre-recorded video of their singing or rapping skills, in document form.
The videos will be watched and judged by our judges during the live event and the result will be declared at the end of it.

Date- 30th October, 2021
Venue- Zoom/google meet
Time- 4:00pm-4:45pm

The submission process for the videos will stop at midnight on 28th October.

The winner will receive a certificate, customised t-shirt and a key chain.  

ART COMPETITION

For this competition, the theme of the artwork will be “Women Empowerment Through Your Eyes”.
As the theme suggests, you’ll have to portray women empowerment, in your artwork, the way you see it.
For the submission process, you’ll have to take a good picture of your artwork and send it in document form, on our gmail account. Digital art as well as Traditional art is allowed. Traditional art is to be presented on an A4 size sheet of paper only.

Date- 30th October, 2021
Venue- zoom/google meet
Time- 4:50pm- 5:10pm

The submission process will stop at midnight on 28th October.

The winner will receive a certificate, customised t-shirt and a key chain. 

KBC (Koun Banega Champion)

KBC will be a live game event, based on general knowledge questions, similar to the KBC( Koun Banega Crorepati) show which actor Amitabh Bachchan hosts on Sony Entertainment Television channel.

The rules of the game will be explained to the participants in the live event itself.

Date- 30th October, 2021
Venue- Zoom/google meet
Time- 5:15pm- 6:00pm

The winner will receive a certificate, customised t-shirt and a key chain.

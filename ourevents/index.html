#inconversationwith Priyanjul and Agniva

Being a gay couple in a country like India is a very difficult thing. Even with the rapidly evolving LGBTQ+ rights in India, there are still many community members who face social mistreatment and legal difficulties that aren’t commonly faced by the cis population.
Priyanjul Johari(he/him) and Agniva Das(he/him) are romantic partners living together in India. In this video they share with us, their journey and the struggles they've faced throughout as an out and proud couple and how the situation stands for the lgbtq community in this country.
